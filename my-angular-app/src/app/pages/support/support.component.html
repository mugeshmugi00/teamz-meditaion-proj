To start your first meditation session, simply log into your account and navigate to the "Start Session" page. Choose a beginner-friendly course like "Foundations of Mindfulness" and follow the guided instructions. Make sure you're in a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed.

You don't need any special equipment to start meditating! A quiet space, comfortable seating (a chair or cushion), and headphones (optional but recommended) are all you need. Our platform works on any device with internet access.

For beginners, we recommend starting with 5-10 minutes per day and gradually increasing to 20-30 minutes. Consistency is more important than duration. Our courses provide structured guidance to help you build a sustainable practice.
