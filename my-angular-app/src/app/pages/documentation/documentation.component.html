Your First Meditation Session

Starting your meditation practice can feel overwhelming, but we've made it simple:

  • Choose a quiet, comfortable space where you won't be disturbed
  • Set aside 5-10 minutes for your first session
  • Follow the guided instructions in your chosen course
  • Don't worry about doing it "perfectly" - consistency is key

Troubleshooting

Audio Issues

If you're experiencing problems with audio during meditation sessions:

  • Check your device volume and audio settings
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try using different headphones or speakers

Account Access

Having trouble accessing your account?

  • Use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset your password
  • Check your email for account verification links
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try accessing from a different browser or device

Progress Not Saving

If your meditation progress isn't being recorded:

  • Ensure you're logged into your account
  • Complete sessions fully (don't close early)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Wait a few minutes for sync to complete
